of self- empowerment among persons with disability.

Georgia Gives Day is a “flash mob of giving” focusing on one day. This year that day is on November 13, 2014. Please consider making a donation to ABT and become a partner to help us further our mission.

This year the proceeds of the Santa’s Sleigh Fund will go to purchasing Strike Force Powerchairs for our Power Soccer team, the North Georgia Screamin’ Eagles. ADA Memorial 5k

This event started in 1997 to celebrate and raise awareness of the Anniversary of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), to increase community wellness, and educate about the barriers in our society facing persons with disabilities. The 5k has become the main fundraiser for our organization.

Head Hawg Tournament

Our Head Hawg tournament started in 2012 at the same time as the Gainesville Jaycees started the Hawgin’ on Lanier BBQ Competition. The team of Jonathan Benton and Tito Salcido won the Doubles Team Division defeating defending Champions Luke and Timothy Pressley in the finals. Timothy won the Male Division defeating cousin and defending Champion Luke Pressley in the finals, and Sandy Roberts took home the trophy in the Female Division. Join us next year for a fun time the Laurel Summerfest sponsored by Hall County Parks & Leisure.
